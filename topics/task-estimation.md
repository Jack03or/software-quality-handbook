# Task Estimation in Scrum

## Overview
Task estimation in Scrum helps the team discuss work before it starts and gives a rough idea of the effort involved.

## Why It Matters
- Helps the team plan sprint work
- Makes large or unclear tasks more obvious
- Supports better discussion before development starts
- Improves consistency in how work is planned

## Best Practices
- Estimate as a team
- Break large tasks into smaller ones
- Use relative estimation instead of exact hours
- Review estimates based on previous sprint experience

## Bad Practices to Avoid
- Letting one person estimate everything
- Treating estimates as fixed promises
- Estimating tasks that are too big or unclear
- Ignoring uncertainty

## Common Challenges
- Unclear requirements
- Different experience levels in the team
- Pressure to give exact deadlines
- Overconfidence in estimates
